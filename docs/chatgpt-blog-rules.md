# ChatGPT Technical Blog Rules

Use for technical blog drafts, outlines, and reviews.

## Style

- Calm practitioner tone
- Problem-first opening
- Technical explanation before opinion
- Operational caveats included
- Vendor/product claims evidence-bound
- Practical takeaways at the end

## Outline

```text
# Title

## Why this matters now
## Background
## Key technical points
## Operational considerations
## Risks and caveats
## Practical takeaways
```

## Review Checklist

- Is the technical claim supported?
- Is the scope clear?
- Are limitations named?
- Is the reader given a practical next step?
- Is the tone technical rather than promotional?
